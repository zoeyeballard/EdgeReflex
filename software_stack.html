WCET instrumentation
  • DWT->CYCCNT ARM cycle counter register
  • GPIO toggle macros task entry/exit markers
  • UART timestamp log per-inference latency
DWT->CYCCNT is the ARM Cortex-M4 hardware cycle counter — it runs at the core clock frequency (80 MHz on TM4C) and costs zero CPU overhead to read. This is your primary WCET measurement tool and the methodology your paper describes.
